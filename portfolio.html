Librepoker 

Librepoker is a free and open-source internet poker server run by University of Toronto Computer Poker Research Group. Users of the site can play online poker anonymously and optionally register an account to play rated games. Librepoker is ad-free and all the features are available for free, as the site is funded by donations from University of Toronto. Features include poker puzzles, computer analysis, tournaments and poker variants.

Poker CAPTCHA 

The Poker CAPTCHA is a service that verifies humans on the internet by making them solve poker puzzles. The poker puzzles consist of choosing winning poker hands from a group of them.

Pokerkit 

Pokerkit is the most complete and feature-rich poker library to be released with a open-source license. Its Python programmatic interface allow users to generate, validate, and apply moves to any poker game state. It supports over 30 poker variants and can easily be extended to support more.

Standard Poker Interface 

The open communication protocol proposed by the University of Toronto Computer Poker Research Group. It enables poker engines to communicate with user interfaces. It improves upon the Universal Poker Interface developed by the PIOSolver team.

Standard Poker Notation 

The poker notation system proposed by the University of Toronto Computer Poker Research Group. It is the most versatile, computer-friendly, and human-readable poker notation system in the world.
